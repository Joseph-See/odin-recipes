Classic Bacon Sandwich

Decription

A simple yet delicious recipe for a bacon sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 150 grams of bacon
  • Sliced bread loaf
  • One tablespoon of butter
  • BBQ sauce
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 210deg C
  2. Place bacon on baking paper lined tray then place in oven for 20 minutes, checking regularly
  3. While bacon is cooking, butter both slices of bread
  4. Once bacon is cooked, place on bread and add sauce and mustard to taste
