Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This is a simple recipe for a delicious spaghetti bolognese.

This recipe serves 2-4 people depending on portion sizes. You can replace spaghetti with any other similar pasta that you like!

Ingredients

  • 500g ground mince
  • 700g jar of pasta sauce
  • 200g spaghetti
  • Garlic, minced
  • Worcestshire sauce
  • One tablespoon olive oil
  • One tablespoon sugar
  • One tablespoon of salt
  • Two tablespoons of tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Bring a saucepan of water to the boil on high heat, and add the salt
  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick saucepan on medium-high heat
  3. Add spaghetti to boiling water. Cover and allow to simmer for around 8 minutes or until al dente.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, stirring regularly
  5. Reduce the heat on the meat to medium-low, and add the pasta sauce while stirring
  6. Add worcestshire sauce, sugar and tomato sauce
  7. Cook for 8 minutes
  8. Drain water from pasta and then serve with the meat sauce
