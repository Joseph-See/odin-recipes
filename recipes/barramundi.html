Air Fried Barramundi

Description

This simple recipe for air fried barramundi is easy to follow, delicious and healthy.

Use of the air frier reduces the amount of oil we need to use for cooking.

Ingredients

  • Two barramundi fillets (skin on or off)
  • One tablespoon of oil
  • One teaspoon of salt
  • One teaspoon of pepper
  • Half a lemon sliced

Steps

  1. Pat dry barramundi fillets with a paper towel.
  2. Coat the fillets with the oil
  3. Rub the fillets with the salt and pepper
  4. Place fillets in the air fryer basket and cook at 180deg C for 20 minutes
  5. Serve with lemon slices
